We at infomatrix are passionate about making news on the internet as accurate as possible. Our team consists of activists who are experts in politics and current events , and are able to dissect news reports via context .For example the word “cockroach” has a different meaning in Rwanda than anywhere in the world. When reviewing media submitted , we assess the context, location, possible implications and stakeholders.
